ABOUT ME

James Cooks

CURRENTLY IN SANTORINI, GREECE

My name is James Smith, and the art of cooking has been my lifelong passion. From the warm aromatic kitchens of India to the cozy countryside bistros of France, I've embraced diverse culinary traditions and techniques. The joy, for me, isn't just in savoring these dishes but in sharing their recipes, stories, and the love that goes into making them. Every place has its unique flavor, and my journey has now brought me to the picturesque landscapes of Santorini, Greece. Here, amidst the whitewashed buildings and sunsets, I am immersing myself in local delicacies and eager to bring these Grecian recipes to your plate
